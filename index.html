Website Logos

Jun 25th, 2014

I was getting some template work done last week and started to have ideas for your logos. I spent about 4 hours designing them, which in the grand scheme of things is really a short time for logo design, but again your budget is pretty small.

Here’s what I wanted to accomplish:

  1. All three had to have elements that were the same, part of the same BIG family
  2. to depict a workshop/industry/DIY feel without being precious
  3. feel hand-printed (screen or an old press)
  4. the ability to print dark on light or light on dark
  5. BIG to be BIG
  6. A draft-like quality for the stylized type to invoke pipes, metal, wood, etc.
  7. Vapor Mechanics logo to feel like it was exhaling

Specs & Qualities:

  • Vector files – perfectly suitable for print and screen
  • Font – Brandon Grotesque – this can also be used as the website’s main font. Examples
  • The draft-like type was created with a mixture of fonts and elements to give it a slightly depression era quality
  • interchangeable typography to help fit the three separate Big entities.
